About SMCC

Our History

St. Mary's College of Catbalogan has been a beacon of Catholic education in Western Samar since 1945. Founded by the Religious of the Virgin Mary (RVM), the institution has been committed to providing quality education rooted in Christian values.

Vision

To be a premier Catholic educational institution in Eastern Visayas, producing competent, morally upright, and service-oriented individuals.

Mission

St. Mary's College of Catbalogan commits itself to provide quality Catholic education that develops the whole person - intellectually competent, morally upright, psychologically mature, physically healthy, socially responsible, and spiritually alive.

Core Values

  • Faith: INITIUM SAPIENTIAE TIMOR DOMINI (The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom)
  • Excellence: Commitment to quality in all endeavors
  • Service: Dedication to community development
  • Integrity: Honesty in all dealings
  • Respect: Compassion for all people

Academic Programs

Basic Education Programs

Kindergarten

Foundation for lifelong learning through play-based and interactive activities.

Grade School (Grades 1-6)

Building strong fundamentals in core subjects and developing essential skills.

Junior High School (Grades 7-10)

Developing critical thinking and preparing students for Senior High School.

Senior High - STEM Strand

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics track for future engineers and scientists.

Senior High - ABM Strand

Accountancy, Business, and Management track for aspiring entrepreneurs and business professionals.

Senior High - HUMSS Strand

Humanities and Social Sciences track for students interested in social work, education, and liberal arts.

Senior High - ICT Strand

Information and Communications Technology track for future IT professionals.

College Education Programs

BS in Computer Science (BSCS)

4-year program preparing students for careers in software development, programming, and IT.

BS in Accountancy (BSA)

4-year program leading to CPA certification and careers in accounting and finance.

BS in Business Management (BSBM)

4-year program developing future business leaders and entrepreneurs.

BS in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

4-year program for careers in hotels, restaurants, and tourism industry.

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)

4-year program preparing future elementary school teachers.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED)

4-year program preparing future secondary school teachers with various specializations.

Admissions

Admission Requirements

For Basic Education:

  • Birth Certificate (Original or Certified True Copy)
  • Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 copies)

For College:

  • High School Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 copies)
  • Medical Certificate

Enrollment Process

  1. Inquiry: Visit the Admissions Office or contact us
  2. Assessment: Take entrance examination (for College applicants)
  3. Submission: Submit all required documents
  4. Payment: Pay enrollment fees
  5. Confirmation: Receive class schedule and student ID

üìÖ Important Dates

Enrollment Period: April - June

Classes Start: First week of June

Entrance Exam: Every Saturday of April-May
